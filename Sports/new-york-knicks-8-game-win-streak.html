The question on everyone's mind is: just what kind of juice is Thibodeau giving these players?

While it's unclear just what has caused this sudden shift in the team's performance, it's clear that whatever Thibodeau has been doing with the players' nutrition has had a positive impact.

Many are curious to know just what kind of "juice" Thibodeau has been giving the Knicks, but for now, it seems that the team is more focused on continuing their winning ways. As long as the Knicks keep racking up victories, it's likely that fans and analysts will continue to speculate about the secret behind their success.

One thing is for certain - the Knicks are a team to watch out for. With their improved performance and newfound winning streak, they could very well make a push for the playoffs. Only time will tell what the future holds for the Knicks, but for now, it looks like they're on the right track.
